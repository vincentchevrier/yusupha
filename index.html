About & Services

With 20 years of professional guiding experience and fluent English, I offer personalized birdwatching experiences across The Gambia's diverse habitats.

The Gambia, known as "The Smiling Coast of Africa," is home to over 500 bird species, making it one of Africa's premier birdwatching destinations. From the mangroves of the coast to the woodland savannas inland, I'll help you discover the remarkable avian diversity this beautiful country has to offer. Tours to Senegal are also available, expanding your birdwatching adventure across the region.
